Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Luxe Events.

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events, and 4-8 weeks for smaller gatherings. However, we accommodate rush requests based on availability — contact us to discuss your timeline.
Each package includes event coordination, décor styling, vendor management, and day-of execution. Higher tiers add photography, premium florals, extended coverage, and dedicated planning teams. Custom add-ons are always available.
We have an exclusive network of trusted vendors offering preferred rates. You're welcome to bring your own vendors, and we'll seamlessly integrate them into the event plan and coordinate on your behalf.
Cancellations made 60+ days before the event receive a 75% refund. 30-59 days: 50% refund. Less than 30 days: deposits are non-refundable. We offer date rescheduling at no additional planning fee when possible.
